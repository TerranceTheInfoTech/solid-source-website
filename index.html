About Solid Source Logistics

Solid Source Logistics LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) based in San Antonio, Texas. We specialize in secure, efficient IT services, cybersecurity, and logistics support tailored for both government and commercial sectors.

With a foundation built on military discipline and technical expertise, our team brings reliable solutions that help organizations stay smart, secure, and streamlined.
