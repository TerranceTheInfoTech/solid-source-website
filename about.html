About Solid Source Logistics

Solid Source Logistics LLC is a Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) headquartered in San Antonio, TX. Founded by Terrance Johnson, a U.S. military veteran and IT professional, SSL delivers high-quality, mission-focused services in Information Technology, Cybersecurity, and Logistics.

We are committed to providing secure, scalable, and smart solutions that support both public sector and private clients. Our values are built on integrity, discipline, and innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge IT and logistics services that support mission-critical operations for federal, state, and commercial clients — with precision, reliability, and a veteran’s mindset.

Founder & Owner

Terrance Johnson brings a background in military service, IT infrastructure, and business logistics. He holds an Associate’s Degree in Information Technology and is completing a Bachelor's Degree in Cybersecurity (expected March 2026).
